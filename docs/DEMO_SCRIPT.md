# Relay Demo Script

## Demo goal
Show that Relay turns raw operational updates into a clear, structured, actionable incident workflow.

## Flow
1. Open Relay demo.
2. Show incident timeline with multiple notes.
3. Show AI summary generated from fragmented observations.
4. Show recommended severity and rationale.
5. Show suggested next actions.
6. Show clean handoff draft for another operator or supervisor.

## What judges should feel
- The product is practical, not gimmicky.
- AI reduces coordination friction.
- The workflow is usable in unreliable-connectivity conditions.
- The app feels like an operational tool, not a toy chatbot.
